1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Identity: Full name, email address, phone number, and postal address when you register or donate.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details processed through Paytm (we do not store card/UPI credentials).
  • Voluntary Information: Messages sent through our contact form, volunteer applications, and feedback.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited (collected automatically).
Note: We never store your full payment card numbers or banking credentials. All payment processing is handled securely by Paytm.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for legitimate charitable purposes:

  • Processing and acknowledging your donations
  • Issuing 80G tax exemption certificates
  • Sending updates about our projects and impact
  • Responding to your queries and communications
  • Improving our website and donation experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: Paytm, to complete your donation transaction.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Trust Operations: With authorised trustees and staff strictly for charitable activities.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Bcrypt password hashing — passwords are never stored in plain text
  • JWT token-based authentication with expiry
  • Regular security reviews and database backups
  • Access restricted to authorised personnel only

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies and session storage for:

  • Session Management: Keeping you logged in during your visit.
  • Preferences: Remembering your theme (dark/light mode) preference.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve site performance.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sadgurubharadwaja.org.

7. Donation Data

Donation records are retained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with Indian accounting and tax regulations (Income Tax Act, FCRA requirements). This data is used exclusively for:

  • Issuing 80G donation receipts and certificates
  • Annual financial reporting and audits
  • FCRA compliance reporting
Donor information included in statutory reports is anonymised wherever possible and handled in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact:

  • Email: info@sadgurubharadwaja.org
  • Organisation: Sri Dutta Sai Manga Bharadwaja Trust
  • Website: sadgurubharadwaja.org

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 10 business days.
